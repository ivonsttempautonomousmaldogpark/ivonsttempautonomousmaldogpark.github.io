Ivon Street Temporary Autonomous Small Dog Park

Tina used to live here. It was a cool old house; it had a little fenced in porch sticking out the side, a baby cage. Tina's gone now, and the house is gone. It's been a vacant lot for months.

Meanwhile, small dogs across inner southeast portland have no place to frolic. Hence the Ivon Street Temporary Autonomous Small Dog Park.

A space where little yappers are treasured.
